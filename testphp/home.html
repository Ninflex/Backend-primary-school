ST Alphanous RC Primary School

Welcome to St. Alphonsus R.C. Primary School. This is a school where our children enjoy their learning, the staff work hard to establish a caring Christian community and everyone is valued equally.

History

St. Alphanous RC Primary School stands as a cornerstone of educational excellence in its community, tracing its roots back to its founding in 1967. Named after the revered Saint Alphanous, known for wisdom and compassion, the school began humbly, fostering a nurturing environment for children to grow academically and spiritually. Over the years, it has evolved into a beacon of learning, embracing modern pedagogy while preserving its rich traditions. With a dedicated faculty and a commitment to holistic development, St. Alphanous RC Primary School continues to empower generations, shaping bright futures and instilling values that extend far beyond the classroom.
